Microsurfacing:

 * Clean the road surface of debris and loose materials.

 * Apply a slurry seal with a mixture of asphalt emulsion (Cold Mix Asphalt can be used), aggregate, and water.

 * Compact the microsurfacing mixture for optimal adhesion.



Chip Seal:

 * Apply a layer of emulsion to the road surface.

 * Cover the emulsion with aggregate chips.
 
 * Compact the surface to embed the chips into the emulsion.



Overlay:

 * Clean the existing road surface.

 * Apply a new layer of asphalt over the existing road.

 * Compact the overlay for a smooth and durable surface.
